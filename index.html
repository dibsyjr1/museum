The Sussex Hisory Museum was founded in 1908 by John Stevens, a man who loved History. It is situated just outside of Brihton and Hove on the A27 towards Falmer. Since its original opening the Museum has undergone several changes and expansions in order to further accomodate new discoveries and although it originally started off showing items exclusively from the 1850s-1950s it has since begun showing off more from other centuries. If you would like to learn more about the Sussex History Museum then please feel free to navigate this site and if you would like to visit and see the exhibits then please look to the 'Visit' page for informaion on opening hours.
